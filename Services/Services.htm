ADI Solutions specializes in the PeopleSoft HRMS suite of client/server and PeopleSoft Internet Architecture applications. We provide contract consultants with technical skills, with particular expertise in PeopleTools, PeopleCode, and SQR.

Maintaining working relationships with a cadre of other highly skilled independent PeopleSoft consultants, we have a growing number of resources available for development projects. Our consultants possess hands-on technical experience, as well as excellent communications and people skills that allow them to quickly become productive members of any project team. They bring the kind of expertise and tools needed to help you build a successful business system. Headquartered in New Jersey, we are currently working with Fortune 500 companies.

Highly skilled in using PeopleTools and PeopleCode, we undertake all tasks, from making simple modifications to designing and building complex new functionality, with the same focused approach on productivity.

We offer customized solutions to our clients in completing their projects. These solutions are meant to provide utmost quality service in the best cost-effective way. In order to achieve this, we have flexibility of providing a mix of on-site/off-site/off-shore services without compromising on quality. Our team of experts has varied skills spanning project management, functional analysis, technical skills, documentation and user training to complete full-cycle implementation, upgrade and maintenance of PeopleSoft, ORACLE, SAP and any other open systems.
